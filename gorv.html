Best Actresses of India

1. Deepika Padukone - One of India's most successful and versatile actresses. She has delivered blockbuster hits and critically acclaimed performances in movies like Padmaavat, Chhapaak, and Piku.
2. Priyanka Chopra - A global icon known for her performances in Bollywood and Hollywood. She has starred in Barfi!, Bajirao Mastani, and The White Tiger, and also won the Miss World title in 2000.
3. Alia Bhatt - A talented actress known for her powerful performances. Her roles in Raazi, Gully Boy, and Gangubai Kathiawadi have cemented her place as one of Bollywood's top actresses.
4. Kangana Ranaut - Famous for her bold roles and strong acting skills. She has won multiple National Awards for films like Queen, Manikarnika, and Tanu Weds Manu.
5. Vidya Balan - Known for her impactful performances in female-centric films such as Kahaani, The Dirty Picture, and Tumhari Sulu.
6. Kareena Kapoor Khan - A versatile actress with a long and successful career, starring in films like Jab We Met, Kabhi Khushi Kabhie Gham, and Good Newwz.
7. Aishwarya Rai Bachchan - A timeless beauty and a skilled actress known for Devdas, Guru, and Jodhaa Akbar. She also won Miss World in 1994.
8. Sridevi - A legendary actress who left a lasting impact on Indian cinema with classics like Chandni, Mr. India, and English Vinglish.
9. Madhuri Dixit - Famous for her exceptional dancing skills and acting prowess, delivering memorable performances in Hum Aapke Hain Koun..! and Devdas.
10. Anushka Sharma - A critically acclaimed actress and producer, known for NH10, Pari, and Sultan. She has also ventured into production with Paatal Lok and Bulbbul.
11. Taapsee Pannu - An actress known for her unconventional roles in films like Pink, Thappad, and Badla.
